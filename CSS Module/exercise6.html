Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A rich and creamy Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Learn more about this dish on Wikipedia.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan over medium heat until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs with grated Pecorino and Parmesan.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  5. Remove pan from heat, add pasta to pancetta, toss well.
  6. Add egg mixture, stirring quickly with a splash of pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with black pepper, garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
